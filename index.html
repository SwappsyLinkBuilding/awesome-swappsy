About the Project

What is Swappsy?

Swappsy is an all-in-one platform built for SEOs, content marketers, and link builders who want to grow their organic presence through genuine collaborations. Instead of hunting for backlink opportunities across fragmented tools and cold email threads, Swappsy gives you a centralised place to post link exchange requests, discover willing partners, and track every live link - all without leaving the platform.

The idea was born out of a simple frustration: the link building process has always been fragmented, manual, and full of noise. Agencies and solo practitioners spend more time coordinating than actually building. Swappsy changes that by matching you with site owners who are actively looking for the same opportunities - so every conversation starts from mutual intent, not a cold pitch.

At its core, Swappsy is a marketplace of trust. Every user on the platform is there because they want to grow through legitimate, white-hat partnerships. Whether you're after niche editorial links, guest post collaborations, or content swaps with complementary brands, the feed surfaces relevant opportunities based on your niche and domain profile - no spam, no sketchy link farms.

Beyond matching, Swappsy is actively evolving into a full ecosystem for SEO professionals. The roadmap includes native SEO tooling, team collaboration features, and a growing directory of vetted tools - making it not just a link exchange, but the operating system for your entire link building workflow. The goal is simple: if you do link building seriously, Swappsy should be the only tab you need open.

This repository tracks the public roadmap, release notes, and community feedback for Swappsy. Contributions, suggestions, and bug reports are welcome. If you work in SEO and want to shape where this goes, you're in the right place.
